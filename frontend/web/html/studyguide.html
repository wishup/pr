Do your kids know how to study the Bible for themselves? Do they have the keys to unlock the truth?

This 8-week study teaches an easy method to study the Scriptures – in less than 20 minutes a day! Soon enough, your kids will be explaining things to YOU!

The study guide is broken down by three age divisions:

  • Primary7-10
  • Junior11-14
  • Senior15-18

It is designed to be flexible enough for any context – from school and church curriculums to personal study time.

We believe that God’s Word speaks to all of us. We designed a very simple, yet profound study for any student of the Scriptures, regardless of experience or background. Over 40,000 students have participated in the Bible Bee, learning how to understand God's Word, and committing it to memory.

The Study Keys explain what tools the student will be using to understand the Word of God, and how to use them. The Discovery Journal takes the student on a journey through the Scriptures, asking questions designed to help the student learn the tools and discover the Bible for themselves.

Each student will be able to study according to their level of knowledge – answering the questions as simply or proficiently as they want.

For the individuals who want to go even further, the Discovery Journal also provides a more in-depth study with a list of passages and items that set the parameters for the Nationals contest.

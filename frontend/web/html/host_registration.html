Information

During nationals, your help is needed in the "Little Siblings" childcare rooms. This room is designed for children ages 1-6 who need temporary care and supervision while parents accompany contestants into the oral recitation rooms. Children are not allowed in the Judging rooms, therefore Parents with "little siblings" will need help with their non-competing children during that time. Your primary role is to work together with your team to supervise and monitor the safety of children in your care. We will also potentially be offering assistance for children ages 1-6 for contestant families only during semifinals and finals depending on the need.
